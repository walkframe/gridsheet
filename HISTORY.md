GridSheet is a high-functionality spreadsheet UI library built for modern frontend apps. It is React-first, highly extensible, and designed to be adaptable to other frameworks through a wrapper architecture. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Unlike “grid-only” components, GridSheet centers on spreadsheet behaviors: built-in formula support with automatic recalculation, multi-sheet state management, and cross-sheet references for building real spreadsheet-like applications. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

GridSheet also focuses on practical app requirements:
- **Large dataset performance**: an official demo generates **10,000 rows × 100 columns = 1,000,000 cells** to showcase efficient rendering and data handling. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
- **Policy-driven UX and validation**: enforce business rules such as numeric constraints and searchable dropdown selections via policies. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
- **Security controls**: support data masking and clipboard protection patterns for sensitive data. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
